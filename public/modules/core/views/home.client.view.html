How It Works

1. We'll use your Last.fm account to find your favorite records.

2. Play by choosing your favorite of two random records from your collection.

3. Our algorithms will build your charts based on every choice you make. The more you play, the more accurate your charts will be.

4. Your charts are public, share them around!
